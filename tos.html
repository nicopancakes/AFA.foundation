Community Guidelines

This Applies to The Trusted Servers Page. All Servers MUST Meet these Standards:

  • No hate speech, slurs, or discrimination against the Pride Community.
  • Respect everyone's privacy, doxxing is strictly forbidden.
  • Server owners must actively moderate against toxicity.
  • We promote positive visibility and acceptance of our community.
  • We do NOT promote Pedophilia, Zoophilia, vice versa.

By joining any AFA-trusted server, you agree to these standards.
